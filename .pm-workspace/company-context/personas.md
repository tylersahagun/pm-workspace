# User Personas

## Sales Representative

- **Goal:** Close deals faster with better preparation
- **Pain:** Forgets details, manual CRM logging, missed follow-ups
- **Success:** Prepared for every call, CRM always current
- **Key workflows:** Call prep, note review, CRM sync, follow-up reminders

## Sales Leader

- **Goal:** Coach effectively, forecast accurately
- **Pain:** Can't be on every call, reactive coaching
- **Success:** Spots issues early, targeted coaching
- **Key workflows:** Call reviews, pipeline analysis, coaching sessions

## CSM

- **Goal:** Retain and expand through proactive engagement
- **Pain:** Doesn't know problems until too late
- **Success:** Catches risk signals early, smooth handoffs
- **Key workflows:** Health monitoring, renewal prep, escalation handling

## RevOps

- **Goal:** Data quality and process compliance
- **Pain:** Inconsistent logging, unreliable data
- **Success:** Automatic capture, reliable analytics
- **Key workflows:** Data hygiene, reporting, process enforcement
