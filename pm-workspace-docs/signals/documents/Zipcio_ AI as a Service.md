# Phase1: Definition and Acquisition

AI as a Service Structure  
Revenue Intelligence as a Service: using AI to drive your top of funnel.

**Phase1: Definition and Acquisition**

* **Structure**  
  * How it works: We build out your ICP based on data you may or may not have. Create more of your ICP as an audience in Clay, and automate that feed directly into HubSpot for your sales team to work directly.  
  * Progression: This is phase 1 of a journey where we focus on loading TOFU with qualified leads designed around the ICP, followed by phase 2 which focuses on conversion.   
  * Cadence:   
    * *ICP Discovery Meeting (45m):* Analyze existing data in the CRM or define net new ICP for hypothesis  
    * *ICP Review Meeting (30m)*: Review built-out ICP in Lucid with backing data for client review.   
    * *AI Data Review (30m):* Review of Clay table data to see how closely the table matches the ICP defined.   
* **Phase**  
  * Persona Definition and Targeting: Definition of ideal buyer profiles based on firmographic and behavioral data.   
    * *Option 1:* Use historical customer data to establish point of reference for ICP  
    * *Option 2:* Establish ICP net new based on lack of/no customer data  
* **Deliverable**  
  * ICP Visual: ICP generation in Lucid outlining data and channels where ICP digest information  
  * Clay Table: Hypothesis table in Clay pulling sample ICP persona group for initial evaluation  
* **Hourly allotment**  
  * Strategy Time: 3 hours  
  * Persona Buildout: 10 hours+ depending on existence of historical data  
  * Initial Clay Table: 5 hours  
    * Clay Table Optimization \+ 3 hours  
  * CRM Dev: 5 hours  
* **Exit/Acceptance criteria**  
  * Criteria 1: Approval from client on Clay Table sample pull.   
    * Answer the question: do these leads represent your ideal customer?   
  * Criteria 2: Buyer persona visualization matches the approved Clay table data.  
* **Metric for Success**  
  * 80% Qualified in Clay Table: Targeting 80% qualification in the Clay table from client.  
    * *Example:* 8 out of 10 leads should match the defined ICP.  
* **Documentation**  
  * ICP: Visual of ICP \+ backing document outlining firmographic and behavioral data points.   
  * Clay Table Documentation: Loom \+ Documentation on Clay table data points and filtering for targeted ICP.  
* [**Economics**](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1SG25_UBHj-bYPORz-61HlNJXBjdrFpl6gohc5ntzDE4/edit?gid=0#gid=0)  
  * Strategy: This is the highest margin, lowest lift part of the service line.   
  * Productization: This can be productized and templatized pretty easily, and gets easier as we swim in similar industries.   
  * Margin: Targeting 50-60% margin on the front end of the service to account for padding leading into the tighter aspects of the service journey.   
  * Pricing: $4,500 per persona \+ Clay table  
* **Systems**  
  * HubSpot: CRM system used to track and report on ICP historical data, and net new ICP firmographic and behavioral data.   
  * Clay: AI system used to target ICP based on hypothesized firmographic and behavioral data.   
  * LucidChart: Visualization tool to show ICP diagram.  
* **Development**  
  * Integration: Clay x HubSpot integration to transmit data to the CRM directly from Clay.   
  * CRM Dev: Creation of custom properties, record customization, segments, reports, and personas in HubSpot to organize the data model around the ICP. 

**Phase 2: Conversion**

* **Structure**  
  * How it works: We build out sequencing in HubSpot to target these AI enriched leads designed around the ICP with the intent to convert them into a meeting/follow up for a sales rep to work.  
  * Progression: This is phase 2 of the journey where we focus on converting AI enriched leads from TOFU to MOFU.  
  * Cadence:   
    * *Sales Messaging and Positioning (45m):* Understand the tones and anti-tones used in sales outreach for content creation  
    * *Sales Master Guide Review (30m)*: Review detailed Sales Master Guide to ensure it aligns with customer’s revenue engine.   
    * *Sequence and Template Review (30m):* Review of templates and sequences: messaging, cadence, tone, etc.  
* **Phase 2**  
  * Conversion and Nurture: Creation of messaging in sequences to convert AI enriched leads into SQLs  
    * *Option 1:* Reference historical sales activity from closed won customers to analyze tones and anti-tones.  
    * *Option 2:* Start fresh based on new ICP/target  
* **Deliverable**  
  * Sales Master Guide: The full guide which outlines sales methodology, messaging, process, etc.  
  * Sequences with Templates: Two (2) sequences per ICP target, with 6 emails.   
* **Hourly allotment**  
  * Strategy Time: 3 hours  
  * Sales Master Guide: 15 hours  
  * Sequence and Templates: 4 hours per sequence  
  * Sequence Optimization Rounds \= 2 hours per sequence  
  * CRM Dev: 3 hours  
* **Exit/Acceptance criteria**  
  * Criteria 1: Approval from client on sales master guide.   
    * Answer the question: does this guide reflect your entire sales process and ICP?   
  * Criteria 2: Sequence and content is approved by client.   
* **Metric for Success**  
  * *Conversion Rate:* Targeting a 15% conversion rate from sequenced emails.   
* **Documentation**  
  * Sales Master Guide: Visual of ICP, sales process, methodology, tones, anti-tones, etc  
  * Content Library: Folder in HubSpot of sequences and templates.  
* **Economics**  
  * Strategy: This is the phase which needs the most ongoing optimization, this will be the lowest margin phase of the journey.  
  * Productization: This can be productized and templatized pretty easily, and gets easier as we swim in similar industries.   
  * Margin: Targeting 40-50% margin on this service line since we will likely have to cut into the margin for ongoing optimization to ensure client success.  
  * Pricing: $5,000 to create sequence and sales master guide, $1,000 per sequence for ongoing optimization (two sequences per persona).  
* **Systems**  
  * HubSpot: CRM system used to distribute sequence.   
  * Clay: AI system used to target ICP based on hypothesized firmographic and behavioral data.   
  * Supered: Used to host the Sales Master Guide documentation and embed the content directly into web browser.   
* **Development**  
  * Supered x HubSpot: Supered x HubSpot integration to embed content directly into the CRM from the Sales Master Guide.  
  * CRM Dev: Creation of sequences, templates, reporting.

**Phase 3: Revenue Intelligence**

* **Structure**  
  * How it works: We build out the embedded AI sales manager which scores all calls off of an AI rubric, shows process violations, and measures the effectiveness of value props, messaging, and the sales rep directly.  
  * Progression: This is the last phase of the journey which focuses on the performance of the sales engine through revenue intelligence.  
  * Cadence:   
    * *Revenue Intelligence Mapping (45m):* Understand what matters to the revenue leader in terms of measurement, outline processes reps need to stick to, value props and messaging, and the qualification process.   
    * *AI Sales Manager Testing (30m)*: Review the newly created AI sales manager test to understand if scoring, mapping, and suggestive activity is working correctly.   
    * *Rollout \+ Activation (30m):* Get all reps activated with AskElephant.  
* **Phase**  
  * Revenue Intelligence: Creation of AI Sales Manager, fields in HubSpot to track quali, Supered process rules to track violations, and AskElephant agent.  
* **Deliverable**  
  * AI Enriched Scoring Rubric: Rubric to score calls from sales reps directly to push data into HubSpot.  
  * Supered Process Board with Rules: Process board for sales manager to see where reps are violating the sales system.  
  * The Zipcio Sales Agent: AskElephant agent that covers MOFU and BOFU.  
* **Hourly allotment**  
  * Strategy Time: 6 hours  
  * Scoring Rubric: 6 hours  
  * Supered Process Board: 4 hours  
  * Optimization Round \= 3 hours  
  * CRM Dev: 5 hours  
* **Exit/Acceptance criteria**  
  * Criteria 1: Approval from client on revenue intelligence AskElephant Agent   
    * Answer the question: does this agent track and report on my sales process accurately?   
  * Criteria 2: Approval on the scoring rubric.   
    * Answer the question: does the rubric measure sales rep calls accurately?  
  * Criteria 3: Approval on Supered process rules and board.   
    * Answer the question: does the process board measure and report on my sales process in real time?   
* **Metric for Success**  
  * AI Call Score: Targeting 80% of calls scoring a 7 or higher  
  * Objection Score: Targeting 80% of calls scoring a 7 or higher  
  * Value Prop Score: Targeting 80% of calls scoring a 7 or higher  
  * Violations Count: Targeting less than 10 violations per rep per week.  
* **Documentation**  
  * Sales System Documentation: Documentation of sales system delivered in Supered which powers the process rules and board.  
  * Scoring Rubrics: Documentation on scoring methodology, point systems, positive/negative values, etc.  
* **Economics**  
  * Strategy: This is the phase which will yield actual revenue results for the customer, and will take serious ongoing optimization.  
  * Productization: This is very bespoke per customer and will be difficult to productize since it’s a connection point between three (3) different systems.  
  * Margin: Targeting 40-50% margin on this service line since we will likely have to cut into the margin for ongoing optimization to ensure client success.  
  * Pricing: $5,000 to create scoring rubrics (value prop, objection, and macro AI call score), AskElephant agent with mapping, and Supered process board with rules.  
* **Systems**  
  * HubSpot: CRM system used to track and measure deals and quali.   
  * AskElephant: AI system used to send data from calls to HubSpot CRM.  
  * Supered: Used to host the process documentation, rules, and board.   
* **Development**  
  * AskElephant x HubSpot: AskElephant x HubSpot integration to send the quali and call data straight to HubSpot.  
  * CRM Dev: Creation of custom properties, reports, and record customization.

## **AI as a Service**

### **Revenue Intelligence as a Service**

**Using AI to help you get more of the right leads at the top of your funnel**

## **Phase 1: Definition and Lead Acquisition**

### **How It Works**

We help you figure out who your ideal customer is, even if you don’t already have good data.

We use AI tools to find more people like that ideal customer and send them straight into HubSpot so your sales team can follow up.

This is the first step of a larger journey:

* **Phase 1:** Fill the top of your funnel with the right leads

* **Phase 2:** Turn those leads into booked calls and customers

---

### **Meeting Schedule**

* **ICP Discovery Meeting (45 minutes)**

   We review your CRM data or create a new ideal customer profile if no data exists.

* **ICP Review Meeting (30 minutes)**

   We show you a visual of your ideal customer and explain the data behind it.

* **AI Data Review (30 minutes)**

   We review real lead data from Clay to see how well it matches your ideal customer.

---

### **What We Do in This Phase**

**Persona Definition and Targeting**

We define who your ideal buyer is using company details and behavior.

You have two options:

* **Option 1:** Use past customer data to define your ideal customer

* **Option 2:** Create a brand-new ideal customer profile if you don’t have data

---

### **What You Receive**

* **ICP Visual**

   A clear diagram showing who your ideal customer is and where they get information.

* **Clay Table**

   A sample list of leads that match your ideal customer for review.

---

### **Time Breakdown**

* Strategy: 3 hours

* Persona Buildout: 10+ hours (depends on existing data)

* Initial Clay Table: 5 hours

* Clay Table Improvements: 3 hours

* HubSpot Setup: 5 hours

---

### **Exit / Approval Criteria**

We move forward when:

1. You confirm the sample leads look like your ideal customer

2. The buyer persona visual matches the approved lead data

---

### **Documentation Provided**

* **ICP Documentation**

   Visual and written explanation of company and behavior traits.

   This phase has high margins and low effort compared to later phases.

* **Pricing:**

   $4,500 per persona, including the Clay table.

---

### **Systems Used**

   Tracks customer data, personas, and reports.
