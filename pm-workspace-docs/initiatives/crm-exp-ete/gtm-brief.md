# GTM Brief: CRM Experience End-to-End

## Value Proposition

**One-liner:** AskElephant's CRM Agent automatically updates your HubSpot after every call—with the confidence and visibility you need to trust automation.

**Extended:** Stop spending hours on manual CRM updates. AskElephant's CRM Agent analyzes your calls, updates HubSpot automatically, and keeps you in control with real-time visibility, smart approvals, and proactive alerts when something needs attention.

---

## Target Segment

### Primary: RevOps Admins at Mid-Market B2B Companies

**Profile:**

- 50-500 employee companies
- HubSpot users (Salesforce later)
- Managing CRM data quality for sales teams
- Frustrated with manual data entry compliance
- Looking for automation they can trust

**Pain Points:**

- Reps don't update CRM consistently
- Data quality issues hurt forecasting
- Existing automation tools are too complex
- No visibility into what automation is doing
- Fear of "black box" AI making mistakes

### Secondary: Sales Leaders

**Profile:**

- Managing teams of 5-50 reps
- Need accurate pipeline data for forecasting
- Want coaching insights from calls
- Time-constrained, need efficiency

**Pain Points:**

- Can't trust CRM data for forecasting
- Reps spend too much time on admin work
- No visibility into what's happening in calls

### Tertiary: Individual Sales Reps

**Profile:**

- Carrying quota
- On 5-10+ calls per day
- Hate manual CRM updates
- Want to focus on selling

**Pain Points:**

- CRM updates feel like busy work
- Forget to log important details
- Miss follow-up tasks

---

## Key Messages

### For RevOps Admins

1. **"Automation you can trust"** - Test before you activate, see exactly what's happening, get alerts when something's wrong.

2. **"Set up in minutes, not hours"** - Pre-built templates for common call types. No prompt engineering required.

3. **"Full visibility, full control"** - Activity dashboard shows every update. You're always in the loop.

4. **"Proactive, not reactive"** - AskElephant alerts you to anomalies before they become problems.

### For Sales Leaders

1. **"Accurate pipeline data, automatically"** - Every call updates HubSpot. No more chasing reps for updates.

2. **"Trust your forecast"** - When CRM data is reliable, forecasting becomes predictable.

3. **"Free your reps to sell"** - Less admin work means more selling time.

### For Sales Reps

1. **"Your CRM updates itself"** - Focus on the conversation, not the data entry.

2. **"Never miss a follow-up"** - Tasks created automatically from your calls.

3. **"You're still in control"** - Approve changes before they happen, or let it run automatically.

## Competitive Positioning

### vs. Manual CRM Updates

- **Problem:** Time-consuming, inconsistent, error-prone
- **AskElephant:** Automatic, consistent, accurate

### vs. Basic Meeting Transcription (Otter, Fireflies)

- **Problem:** Transcripts don't update CRM
- **AskElephant:** Direct CRM integration with intelligent field mapping

### vs. Gong/Chorus

- **Problem:** Expensive, complex, focused on coaching not CRM
- **AskElephant:** Purpose-built for CRM automation, simpler, more affordable

### vs. Zapier/Make Automations

- **Problem:** Requires technical setup, no AI understanding
- **AskElephant:** AI-powered, understands conversation context, no-code setup

### vs. Native HubSpot AI

- **Problem:** Limited capabilities, not conversation-aware
- **AskElephant:** Deep meeting analysis, cross-platform, more intelligent
